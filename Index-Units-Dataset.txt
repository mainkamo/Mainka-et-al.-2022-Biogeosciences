Explanation of the units in Data_Merced_Chronosequence.csv
Linked to the Mainka et al. (2022) Dataset.

Soil age: ka
mean depth: cm
d15N: per mille
N: g/cm²
d13C: per mille
C: g/cm²
FTIR-values: Absorbance units
Clay: percent, <2 micrometer
Silt: percent, 2-53 micrmeter
Sand: percent, > 53 micrometer
Al: mg/kg
Al2O3: %
Ca: mg/kg
CaO: %
Fe: mg/kg
Fe2O3: %
K: mg/kg
CEC: meq/100g
BaseSaturation: %
TRB: meq/100g
rootC input: mgC/cm³
K2O: %
Mg: mg/kg
MgO: %
Na: mg/kg
Na2O: %
Si: mg/kg
SiO2: %
Ba: mg/kg
Co: mg/kg
Cr: mg/kg
Cu: mg/kg
Mn: mg/kg
MNO: %
Ni: mg/kg
P: mg/kg
P2O5: %
S: mg/kg
Sr: mg/kg
Ti: mg/kg
TiO2: %
Zn: mg/kg
Zr: mg/kg
